Correct Range Logic

Weekly FR/HR/QR/ER = Previous Weekly High − Previous Weekly Low.
Daily FR/HR/QR/ER = Previous Day High − Previous Day Low.
London FR/HR/QR/ER = Previous NY Day High − Previous NY Day Low.
New York FR/HR/QR/ER = Current London High − Current London Low.
